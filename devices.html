Device Collection

Here are all the devices I have collected over the years.

Sony Ericcson K700i

My first phone. Was made to pay for it from my pocket money.

Nokia N900

A present from my dad.

Nokia 808 PureView

The first phone that I bought without having to save up.

Incredible camera and Symbian!

Sony Xperia Z1

Got it for the free bundled headphones. They're doing better than the phone.

Samsung Galaxy Note Edge

The second phone I got in Japan. At last, a stylus!

LG G3 S

Needed for use back home. It was barely usable.

Sony Xperia XZ1 Compact

Never really used it much.

Samsung Galaxy Fold (Gen 1 - 2019)

When the Note Edge died, I put my cards on this. Great phone! Could do with a 3.5mm socket and mSD card slot.

Fujitsu T730

My first tablet, and first Tablet PC. It was snazzy for the time.

Asus Vivotab Note 8

Wonderful, if fragile. A shame small Windows devices are so rare these days.

Samsung Tab A (2015)

Cheap and underpowered. But it's a great tool with wonderful battery life.

Samsung Galaxy Book 10.6

Currently my sole proper computer. Underpowered, but it gets the job done.

It has Wacom EMR. No successor was ever released though.

King Jim Freeno

eInk device that was in firesale. A bit small and poor battery life, but great EMR!

Samsung V20

My first laptop. Good for the time.

Asus... F3Jp?

Perhaps this one. It looks very familiar. I think mine had a HD-DVD drive though.

It served me well. Got long in the tooth though.

Samsung ATIV Book 8

A great laptop that went through so much, including surviving falling off at car roof at about 30mph!

Died of overheating after seven years.

Did you know the 'ATIV' is Vita (like the PS handheld) spelt backwards?

And an amusing blog sales pitch from Japan for the Galaxy Fold.
